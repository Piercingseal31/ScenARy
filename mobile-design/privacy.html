Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is ScenARy's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our app.

1. Information We Collect

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Personalizing your AR experience
  • Analytics to improve app performance

3. Location Data

To provide AR features and location-based content, we require access to your device's location. This data is used only while using the app.

4. Camera Access

ScenARy requires camera access to display Augmented Reality overlays. We do not record or store camera data on our servers.
